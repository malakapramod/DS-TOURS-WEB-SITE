FAQ

Everything you need to know about your Sri Lankan adventure

What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?

The best time to visit depends on the region. For the west and south coasts and the hill country, December to March is ideal. For the east coast, the best weather is from April to September.

Do I need a visa for Sri Lanka?

Yes, most travelers need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) or a visa to enter Sri Lanka. You can apply online before your arrival.

Is it safe to travel in Sri Lanka?

Sri Lanka is generally a safe country for tourists. However, as with any travel destination, it's wise to take standard precautions and stay updated on local news.

What currency is used in Sri Lanka?

The official currency is the Sri Lankan Rupee (LKR). Major credit cards are widely accepted in urban areas, but it's handy to carry cash for small purchases and tips.
